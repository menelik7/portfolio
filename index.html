THE SCOOP

STORY


I am originally from Ethiopia and have been living in the U.S. for half my life. I currently work as a Project Engineer at BenchMark Management LLC, mainly managing vertical construction projects. My job is challenging and requires a good deal of time management. I enjoy what I do, but it does not provide opportunities to be genuinely creative.


PASSION


I have been fascinated with software development dating back to the first time I was introduced to the Commodore 64. The owner's manual included pre-written programs. One rendered miniature hot-air ballons flying across the screen in random directions; and the other, was a segment of a musical keyboard. The rush I felt when the codes worked was indescribable. The fantasy of getting there is even more addictive.

  • This useful app was created as a follow up to my completion of the Modern React with Redux course on Udemy.
